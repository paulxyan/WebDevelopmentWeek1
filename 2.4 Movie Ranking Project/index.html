Best Movies According to Paul

Paul Yan's Top 3 Movies of All Time

Goodfellas

In my opinion one of the best gangster movies of all time, and also depicts the complicated moral lives of people
Growing up in a mafia family in New York. It also captures really well the stories of each character and makes you feel connected to them

Justice League Crisis on Two Earths

One of the greatest DC animated films and is just a fun to watch action movie which contains my favourite villain, Owlman
Watching him justify his actions with his nihilistic views on life made it a more interesting film to watch as grew up

Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

My favourite Harry Potter movie since I think the acting was the best in this movie, also the first time
Death eaters were introduced and we got to see Gary Oldman play Sirius black!
